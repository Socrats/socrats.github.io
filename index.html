Short bio

I am a Postdoctoral researcher (F.W.O. fellow) at the Artificial Intelligence Lab of the Vrije Universiteit Brussel. I am also affiliated with the Machine Learning Group (Université Libre de Bruxelles). I am interested in the origins of cooperation in social interactions and how we can maintain it in an increasingly complex and hybrid human-AI world. I apply concepts and methods from (Evolutionary) Game Theory, Behavioural economics, and Machine Learning to model collective (strategic) behaviour and validate it through behavioural economic Experiments. I am also the creator of EGTtools a Python/C++ toolbox for Evolutionary Game Theory.
